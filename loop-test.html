Plays a 2-second pre-rendered measure on loop via HTMLAudioElement. If it hiccups or pauses at the loop point (every 2 seconds, on beat 1), we hear the problem.

Listen for: a consistent click every 500ms (quarter notes). The downbeat every 2 seconds is higher-pitched. A clean loop means the accent comes on time, every time. A broken loop means you'll hear a stutter or pause at the top of each measure.
