Test protocol
  • Pick a mode. Tap Play. You should hear sine-wave clicks.
  • Tap Switch. Note how long the gap is before you hear wood clicks.
  • Tap Switch again. Then repeat a bunch of times.
  • Switch to next mode, repeat.
  • Log shows ms from tap to audio state change — but trust your ears, they're the real judge.
